Welcome to the English 402 class project presented by Team Breaking Bags!

Our team members - Hingry, Khalefah, Mahdi, Nicholas, Sanghyuk - have collaborated on an innovative project to combat the growing issue of plastic pollution. We focus specifically on single-use plastic bags, a prominent contributor to this global problem.

In our project, we propose to collect unused plastic bags and repurpose them into waterproof blankets. We believe this initiative will not only minimize waste but also foster environmental awareness among students. Additionally, it can offer a practical solution to rainy outdoor situations on campus.

We invite you to join us in this environmental endeavor, reduce plastic waste, and create a more sustainable future for all. Let's Break the Bags!

Environmentally Friendly

  • Transform unused plastic bags into waterproof fabric, mitigating the issue of plastic waste.
  • Promote sustainable lifestyle by encouraging the reuse of materials.

Cost-Effective

  • Minimal implementation cost as most students already possess the necessary materials.
  • Contribute to the reduction of campus waste and carbon footprint by decreasing demand for new products.

Practical

  • Provide a comfortable and dry seating option in the open spaces of WSU campus, enhancing the outdoor experience for students.

Versatile

  • Compatible with nearly all types of fabric, eliminating the need for new blankets purchase.
